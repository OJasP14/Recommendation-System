About Us

Our approach consists of two types of Recommender Systems: Popularity Based and Collaborative Filtering Based. The Popularity Based system suggests the top 50 popular books based on ratings and average ratings. The Collaborative Filtering system recommends the top 10 most similar books using cosine similarity. The popular books dataframe is saved as "popular.pkl".
